Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for the visa?

expand_more

You will typically need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, passport-sized photos, and proof of vaccination (Meningitis ACWY). Our team will provide a detailed checklist upon booking.

Can I pay in installments?

expand_more

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for bookings made at least 3 months in advance. An initial deposit is required to secure your package, with the balance due 4 weeks before departure.

Is the deposit refundable?

expand_more

Deposits are generally non-refundable as they are used to secure flights and hotels immediately. However, we recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unforeseen cancellations.

Do you provide female guides?

expand_more

Yes, we have experienced female scholars and guides available in select groups to assist women with their rituals and questions in a comfortable environment.
