General Overview

What services does Al-Safa Travel provide?

keyboard_arrow_down

We specialize in comprehensive travel packages for Hajj and Omrah pilgrimages. Our services include visa processing, flight bookings, hotel accommodations near the Haram, ground transportation in Saudi Arabia, and guided tours for religious sites (Ziyarat). We also offer customized packages to suit different budgets and group sizes.

Is travel insurance included in the packages?

keyboard_arrow_down

Basic travel insurance is mandated by the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah for visa issuance and is included in your visa fee. However, we strongly recommend purchasing additional comprehensive travel insurance to cover trip cancellations, lost luggage, and medical emergencies beyond the basic coverage.

Do you provide female guides for sisters?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we have experienced female scholars and group leaders available for our larger group packages to assist sisters with religious questions and guidance throughout the rituals of Omrah and Hajj.

Omrah Packages

What is the best time to perform Omrah?

keyboard_arrow_down

Omrah can be performed at any time of the year. However, the months of Rajab, Shaban, and especially Ramadan are considered highly meritorious. If you prefer fewer crowds and cooler weather, we recommend booking during the months of November through February.

Can I customize my Omrah package duration?

keyboard_arrow_down

Absolutely. While our standard group packages are typically 7, 10, or 14 days, we offer "Custom FIT" (Free Independent Traveler) packages where you can choose your travel dates, duration, and specific hotels to match your schedule.

Visas & Documentation

How long does the Omrah visa processing take?

keyboard_arrow_down

With the new electronic visa system, processing is very fast. typically taking between 24 to 72 hours once all required documents are submitted. However, we recommend applying at least 2 weeks before your intended travel date to account for any system delays.

Is the Meningitis vaccination mandatory?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, the Meningococcal Meningitis (ACWY) vaccination is a mandatory requirement for all pilgrims arriving for Hajj and Omrah. You must present a valid vaccination certificate obtained no less than 10 days and no more than 3 years before arrival.

Do I need a Mahram for Omrah?

keyboard_arrow_down

Recent regulations by the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah have eased restrictions. Women aged 45 and over may travel in groups without a Mahram. For women under 45, regulations can vary based on nationality and current visa rules. Please contact our agents for the most up-to-date advice for your specific situation.
